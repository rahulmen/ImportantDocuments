1. REST :- Representation State Transfer
2. Alternative of SOAP Protocol.
3. RESTFUL Webservice can be considered at LightWeight alternative(Performance is higher that SOAP Webservice)
4. REST is not a protocol it is an Architecture.
5. We will use Standard HTTP methods to access the resources. They are GET,PUT,POST and DELETE.
